About FSW Club

Who we are and what we do.

The FSW (Free Software Wing) Club is a student-run organization dedicated to fostering a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts. We believe in the power of collaboration, continuous learning, and hands-on experience. Our club provides a platform for students to explore and contribute across various domains including publicity, event management and logistics, public relations, documentation, tech, design, database, and arts. These diverse areas ensure that every student, regardless of background or interest, finds a place to learn and grow.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application. We organize workshops, hackathons, guest lectures by industry experts, and collaborative projects that allow our members to gain real-world experience and build a strong portfolio. Whether you're skilled in tech, design, documentation, or passionate about publicity, logistics, or the arts, FSW Club is the perfect place to grow your skills, network with peers, and turn your innovative ideas into reality.
